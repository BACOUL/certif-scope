Methodology

This assessment applies a spend-based carbon footprint estimation approach, inspired by the GHG Protocol Corporate Standard.

Key principles

  • Estimation based on financial expenditure data.
  • Sector-based emission factors.
  • Separate treatment of energy-related emissions where applicable.

Emission factors

Emission factors are derived from the ADEME Base Empreinte, a recognized European reference.

Regulatory context

The Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD) applies directly to large companies operating in the European Union.

Under CSRD and the ESRS standards, companies must report Scope 3 emissions, including emissions generated by suppliers and partners.

As a result, SMEs are increasingly requested to provide carbon footprint information to their clients.

The VSME framework provides a simplified and proportionate reporting approach for SMEs in this context.

Legal notice & limitations

This document constitutes a methodological carbon footprint attestation.

It does not constitute a statutory CSRD filing, a certified carbon audit, a regulatory approval or a legally binding environmental certification.

Results are estimates based on standardized methods and the data provided at the time of assessment, intended solely for reporting and pre-compliance purposes.
