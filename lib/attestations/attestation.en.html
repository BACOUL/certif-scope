Methodology overview

This estimate is calculated using a spend-based approach, based on supplier-provided annual spending data and standardized emission factors. No physical activity data is required. No Scope 1, Scope 2 or Scope 3 allocation is performed.

What this attestation is not

  • an audit
  • a regulatory carbon report
  • CSRD or ESRS reporting
  • a Scope 1, Scope 2 or Scope 3 inventory

Legal scope and limits

This document is provided for indicative purposes only. Data accuracy and completeness remain the sole responsibility of the supplier. Certif-Scope does not audit, verify or certify the underlying data.
